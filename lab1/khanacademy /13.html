Recipe of simple sanwhich

  • Time:10 minutes
  • Portions: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 2 slices
Avocado 1
Cherry tomatoes 6
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
Butter to taste

Step 1 : Spread butter to both side of the bread, and toast it until golden brown with low - medium heat or use bread toaster.

Step 2: Meanwhile, mashed avocado and season it with salt and pepper. Cut cherry tomatoes and set aside.

Step 3: Fry egg with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper.

Step 4: Spread mashed avocado to the toasted bread, put cherry tomatoes and fried egg. Enjoy!

Recipe of Yogurt Bread

  • Time:30 minutes
  • Portions: 5 pieces
Ingredients Quantity
Carbquick Flour 3/4 cup
non-fat Greek yogurt 3/4 cup

Step 1 : Mix the flour with the yogurt

Step 2: Until form a dough ball.

Step 3: Use a teaspoon to divide the dough in five pieces coated with melted butter and bake 20min.

Step 4: Let them rest for 15min and enjoy! This bread it’s excellent for soup and sauce recipes.

Recipe of Waffles

  • Time:20 minutes
  • Portions: 10 pieces
Ingredients Quantity
flour 335 g
caster sugar 30 g
baking powder 4 tsp
milk 330 ml
Eggs 4
unsalted butter 150 g

Step 1 : Place the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in a large mixing bowl. Whisk to mix.

Step 2: Add milk, eggs and vanilla. Whisk to combine. Add melted butter then whisk until lump free. The batter should be pourable but thick – slightly thinner than pancakes.

Step 3: Cover then refrigerate overnight, or for at least 2 hours. Softer insides, better flavour. Else, proceed immediately with cooking.

Step 4: Preheat a waffle maker. A non stick one will not need oil, plus there is butter in the batter.

Step 5: Use a ladle to pour batter in to just cover the iron. Don't get greedy – it will leak out the sides! Cook until golden and crisp – my good waffle iron takes 3 1/2 minutes, my Kmart one takes 6 minutes.
